# About our game project

- A collaborative effort by our team to make a turn based (out of turn) card
  game.

## `Organ Attack` **description**:

- player play their turn to defend and afflict damage to organs of each other .

- Non turn player can still defend themselves and can afflict damage using the
  <font color='#b42929'>_**instant cards**_</font> .
